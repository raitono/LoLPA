# LoLPA
League of Legends Performance Analyzer

## What does this site offer?
The site will offer in-depth analysis of match-by-match performance and track user growth over time.

## What makes this site stand out from others like it?
  * Match details will be supplemented with analysis of major performance benchmarks such as Kill/Death/Assist ratio, Gold Earned, Damage Dealt, etc.
  * A relational approach to the analysis of statistical figures will provide a better glimpse of user progression. The goal is not only to determine if the user improved, but by how much.
  * New benchmarks will be developed to assess user performance. It will not be a simple comparison of the numbers generated during a match. Match analysis will take into account the role of the user's Champion in the meta-game and determine how well they performed their role as best as can be determined from the Riot API. A sampling of new benchmarks are defined below. 
    * _Did a Tank Champion perform their role by soaking up damage without dying?_
    * _Did a Healing Champion effectively mitigate enemy damage?_
    * _Did a Carry Champion manage to farm efficiently while hitting their item spikes at appropriate times?_
    * _Did a Jungle Champion control and secure neutral objectives for their team?_
    * _Did a user participate in taking objectives?_
